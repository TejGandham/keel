A. Flow — Uniform Cards

Same-sized cards that wrap with flexbox. Cards get a tinted border based on status. Clean repos are compact; repos needing attention show action buttons. Clicking a card expands it inline (pushes others down).

B. Flow — Adaptive (Chips + Cards)

Clean repos collapse to compact chips (just dot + name + branch). Repos needing attention expand into full cards with details and actions. Information density scales with how much attention a repo needs. "Nothing to see" repos stay out of your way.
